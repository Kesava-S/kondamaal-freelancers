Internship Selection Program

AI Marketing Automation Intern

A structured 3-stage programme to select, train, and place the best CS students as AI Marketing Automation Interns at Automaitee Digital.

How It Works

The programme includes a comprehensive selection process, intensive 1-month remote training, and a final evaluation leading to placement.

Eligibility Criteria (Required)

Applicants must meet all of the following requirements:

  • Currently enrolled in Computer Science, IT, BCA, MCA, or a related degree programme
  • Middle-year undergraduate students or first-year master’s students only
    (Final-semester students are not eligible, as the program requires sufficient time and commitment)
  • Minimum 65% aggregate in current academic performance
  • Minimum 70% attendance in current college
  • Must have a personal laptop (shared or lab computers are not acceptable)
  • Must have a stable internet connection at home for live online sessions
  • Basic understanding of at least one programming language, APIs, or JSON
  • Must be available for 8 hours per week throughout the 1-month course
  • Must attend all live sessions
  • Maximum 1 absence allowed with prior notice before the session starts
  • Missing a session without prior notice will result in immediate disqualification

Preferred Qualifications (Not Mandatory)

These will increase selection chances:

  • Active GitHub profile with personal or academic projects
  • Basic exposure to digital marketing tools (Meta Ads Manager, Google Ads, etc.)
  • Knowledge of JavaScript, APIs, or JSON
  • Experience using AI tools independently (Claude Code, Claude Cowork, Claude Skills, etc.)
  • Any freelance work or side projects showing initiative and self-driven learning

May Reduce Selection Chances

These do not disqualify, but may reduce selection chances if competition is high:

  • No coding exposure
  • No GitHub or personal portfolio
  • Academic performance below 65%
  • College attendance below 75% (may indicate lower commitment)

Automatic Disqualification (No Exceptions)

Applicants will be automatically rejected if any of the following apply:

  • Not from CS, IT, BCA, MCA, or related field
  • Final-semester student with less than 6 months remaining
  • Academic aggregate below 65%
  • College attendance below 70%
  • No personal laptop
  • No stable home internet connection
  • No basic coding knowledge
  • Missing a live session without prior notice during the course

This opportunity is designed for committed students who are willing to learn quickly and actively participate throughout the program.

What we expect from you

This programme demands genuine commitment. Live sessions are mandatory. You are allowed a maximum of one absence during the course, and only if you inform us before the session begins. Missing a session without prior notice results in immediate removal from the programme — no exceptions and no refund.

We are not strict for the sake of being strict. We are strict because you will be working with real clients and real money. Professional discipline starts here.

You must agree to the expectations to proceed.

Course fee

₹1,000 — one-time, non-refundable

Shortlisted candidates will have to pay 1000 rupees to enroll the course.

This covers your access to live sessions, real ad platform accounts, course materials, and task evaluations throughout the 1-month course. The fee is intentionally kept low to make this accessible. It is non-refundable because you are receiving real tool access and expert instruction from day one.

You must confirm you understood the fee structure.

Frequently asked questions

  • Q: Is this a certificate course? A: No. This is a selection programme for an internship. You do not receive a certificate. You receive real skills, real experience, and if you perform well, a role on our team.
  • Q: What happens if I am not selected after the course? A: You will receive a personalised email explaining why and what to work on. The ₹1,000 fee is non-refundable — you received a full month of live training on real platforms regardless of selection outcome.
  • Q: Can I attend if I am from a college not mentioned on the form? A: Yes. The programme is open to eligible students from any college as long as you meet the eligibility criteria.
  • Q: Will sessions be recorded? A: Yes. All live sessions are recorded and shared with enrolled participants only. However attendance to live sessions is still mandatory — recordings are for revision only, not a substitute for being present.
  • Q: When does the programme start? A: Applications are open until seats are filled. Once the batch is confirmed, selected students will receive the schedule by email.
You must confirm you are clear on the FAQs.
