Frequently Asked Questions

For Employers

What does "monitored freelancer" mean?

Every freelancer is guided by an experienced Kondamaal mentor. The mentor ensures project alignment, workflow design, and adherence to timelines.

Is it safe to share our business data?

Yes. We implement strict NDAs, data flow principles, and secure deployment methods so your sensitive data never leaves your infrastructure unsupervised.

For Talents

How do I apply if there isn't a job listing?

We only accept talent through scheduled problem-solving research challenges. You can express interest on our contact page to get notified of the next intake round.

Are the positions paid?

Absolutely. Once trained and matched with a client, our freelancers earn competitive rates, gaining real-world experience and payment right away.
